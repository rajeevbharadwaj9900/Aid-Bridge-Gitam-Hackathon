Food Donation Aid Details

Describe the resources needed for food donations in the community:
For food donation initiatives in our community, we require non-perishable food items such as canned goods, rice, pasta, grains, and other essential food staples. Additionally, we need volunteers who can help collect, sort, and distribute the donated food items to individuals and families in need.
Describe the statistics about food insecurity in our community:
In our community, approximately 20% of households experience food insecurity, meaning they lack reliable access to a sufficient quantity of affordable, nutritious food. This issue affects individuals of all ages, including children and seniors, and is exacerbated by factors such as unemployment, poverty, and limited access to grocery stores and fresh produce.
